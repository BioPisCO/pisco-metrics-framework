Collection of Biological Resource Metrics

Provides evidence of the resource impact in publications saved in Pubmed Central (PMC) repository

 

API: NCBI API (E-utilities)

Query: Keyword separated by comma. Includes all words and numbers in the title, abstract and article body, as well as in table and figure captions and in the article reference section

Output: Total publication that includes the keyword
